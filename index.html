Frequently Asked Questions

"Periyar for Youth" is an initiative dedicated to empowering young individuals through skill development programs inspired by the vision of Thanthai Periyar. Our mission is to provide accessible education and resources to foster personal and professional growth among the youth.
Our programs are open to all youth who are eager to learn and develop new skills. There are no specific prerequisites or qualifications required to join.
We offer a variety of classes, including:
  • Photography Classes: Learn the fundamentals of photography, camera handling, and photo editing.
  • Basics of Computers: Get introduced to computer operations, internet usage, and essential software applications.
  • English Language: Improve your spoken and written English for effective communication.
  • Resume Writing: Learn how to craft a professional resume to enhance your job prospects.
